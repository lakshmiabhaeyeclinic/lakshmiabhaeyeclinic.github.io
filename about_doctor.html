About the Doctor

Meet Our Experienced Retina Specialist

Dr. Prajjwal Kumar

Dr. Prajjwal Kumar is a highly skilled and experienced Retina Surgeon with more than 15 years of experience in the field of ophthalmology. He specializes in the diagnosis and treatment of retinal diseases using advanced medical and surgical techniques.


Throughout his career, Dr. Prajjwal Kumar has successfully treated thousands of patients suffering from retinal disorders, diabetic retinopathy, macular diseases, and other complex eye conditions. His commitment to excellence, compassionate care, and use of modern technology ensure that every patient receives the highest quality of eye care.
